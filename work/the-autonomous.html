The Autonomous

A two-day international event on safe and secure software-defined autonomy, with technical workshops, C-level keynotes, and expert panels exploring the present and future of autonomous technology. I led its year-round LinkedIn content.

The Autonomous brings the industry together to explore where autonomous technology is heading. My role was to carry that conversation across the year on LinkedIn, keeping the audience engaged long before and after the two days themselves.

I led LinkedIn content execution for year-round event promotion, creating detailed design briefs that defined messaging, content flow, and visual direction. Day to day, that meant coordinating publishing, monitoring engagement trends, and creating and refining content based on audience response and performance insights.
